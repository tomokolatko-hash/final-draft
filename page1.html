About me

I am a Japanese ceramic artist currently studying ceramics at San Jose State University. My work focuses on shedding light on the often-overlooked details in daily life, drawing out beauty from the ordinary. Even in practical pieces such as vases and tableware, I prioritize not only their functional aspects but also the visual impact and tactile pleasure brought by their form and texture. Through this, I hope to encourage people to recognize the importance of integrating art into their everyday lives.
