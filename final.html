Art 74 Final Project

For the final assignment, I decided to combine the skills and knowledge I’ve learned in class to create a ceramics portfolio that also functions as an online shop. I’ve always wanted to design my own website but didn’t know how until taking this class. Learning HTML and digital design has allowed me to bring both physical and digital creativity together into a platform where I can showcase my ceramics. I created another website for my final project. Additionally, I incorporated a few p5.js games related to ceramics and creating a 3D image using Blender. As a ceramic artist and I am deeply passionate about my work. I feel more comfortable with physical materials than digital tools, so this assignment is a special opportunity for me to explore a new medium. I’m excited to showcase the ceramic pieces I’ve created so far and to apply the skills I’ve learned in class to this project. I believe that motivation and passion are essential when creating something meaningful, and this assignment offers a wonderful chance for everyone in class to express their artistic ideas freely and explore personal interests.
