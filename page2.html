Glitch

Glitch is a form of artistic expression that transforms inner emotions and mistakes, and accidents into meaningful art. I made those glitched arts using photos of my ceramic work. Ceramics are usually nicely formed and shaped for use, but I changed the images to make them look broken or strange. I liked how glitched images can make something new and they look still beautiful in a new way. My glitch arts show how both real images and glitched images can merge and mixed and still be interesting. I think that the imperfection can be creative and meaningful.
