MEME Mashup Art Restration went wrong

I created the video with the MEME of failed art restorations like the famous “Ecce Homo” painting. They became so famous and spread as MEME in the world so I made a mashup to show collection of those failed arts could be another new wave. Those painters were meant to fix old works but ended up creating something odd and strange, destroying the art piece and also the important art history. My video explores how collections of failures can be funnier art and those fails even become more famous than the original art….sad but true.. My MEME mashup is based on how we see humor and mistakes in today’s art world.
